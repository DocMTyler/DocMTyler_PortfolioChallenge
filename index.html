About...


Welcome. My entire life has been consumed by my fascination with learning about the world. After high school, I went into Psychology to learn more about neurons and how they work. After my undergraduate work, I focused on cellular and systems neurophysiology. I received my PhD in 2013. Currently, I am focused on learning how neural netwoks can be represented in digital systems and artificial intelligence, though not exclusively. I have travelled extensively through Asia, and lived in China from 2017 until 2020, and after China in Thailand for 3 months. I am an intermediate speaker of Mandarin and German, and am very passionate about languages and how our brains process them. I have many other interests. If I have piqued yours, then I encourage you to take a look around here and talk to me. Cheers.
